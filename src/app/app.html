Sashi Obilisetty

Co-Founder & CEO

Sashi Obilisetty is an EDA technologist with extensive entrepreneurial experience and a technical leader with deep expertise across AI, cloud, and the full chip design lifecycle. Before launching her latest startup, she served as Executive Director of R&D at Synopsys. During her 15+ year tenure, her achievements included establishing a company‑wide AI organization that enabled AI capabilities across the entire product portfolio, leading R&D teams in front‑end verification and post‑silicon analytics, and most recently guiding the technical strategy for Synopsys’s multi‑billion‑dollar EDA business.

From 2020 to 2021, Sashi was the Chief Architect for Silicon Solutions at Google Cloud, shaping cloud‑scale infrastructure for advanced silicon programs and accelerating the adoption of chip design on the cloud.

She previously founded two EDA startups, grounding her leadership in fast iteration, customer‑focused innovation, and a builder’s instinct for turning ideas into execution. Her career spans nearly every stage of semiconductor development and includes solving complex problems across design, verification, and silicon bring‑up.

Sashi is the 2024 Marie Pistilli Women in EDA Award recipient, recognized for her impact on the industry and her advocacy for AI‑native design flows. She holds multiple patents in EDA and AI and brings a rare combination of startup agility, enterprise‑scale leadership, and deep technical breadth across domains.

Radha Srinivasan

Co-Founder & Vice President of Engg

Radha is a co‑founder of CaretEDA, where she focuses on applying AI, cloud workflows, and open‑source tools to make chip design more efficient. Her work centers on simplifying complex engineering steps so teams can move from early concepts toward silicon with fewer bottlenecks.

Before starting CaretEDA, Radha spent almost 20 years at Synopsys working across platform engineering, AI, and Silicon Lifecycle Management analytics. Her contributions included developing cloud‑based systems for monitoring chip health and reliability, as well as supporting the growth, quality, and long‑term maintainability of several widely used EDA tools.

Her technical approach draws heavily on clear, streamlined solutions. She has experience spanning low‑level hardware considerations, data‑driven reliability modeling, large‑scale distributed systems, and the practical challenges of deploying production‑grade EDA infrastructure. This background helps shape CaretEDA’s engineering direction, particularly in areas such as autonomous design agents, scalable cloud orchestration, and integrating open‑source components into reproducible, end‑to‑end flows.

Radha is an IEEE Senior Member and has been involved in community efforts such as chairing the Technical Program Committee for IEEE WINTECHCON and presenting at IEEE Conferences.

Surajprakash Ankush
